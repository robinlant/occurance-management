How It Works

DutyRound sends email notifications automatically in the background.

New Occurrence

Participants receive a digest of available occurrences with open spots.

Unfilled Spots (Participants)

Participants are notified about upcoming occurrences that still need people.

Unfilled Spots (Organizers)

Organizers receive a heads-up about upcoming occurrences with free places.

Note: Admins do not receive any notification emails. The "Max Emails Per Day" setting prevents spamming users.
